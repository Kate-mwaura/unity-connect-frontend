About Unity SACCO

Building Financial Futures Together

For over 25 years, Unity SACCO has been dedicated to empowering our members with trusted financial services and building stronger communities through cooperative banking.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional financial services that empower our members to achieve their dreams while building stronger, more prosperous communities through cooperative principles.

Our Vision

To be the leading SACCO in Kenya, recognized for our innovation, member-centric approach, and commitment to sustainable community development.

Our Values

Integrity, transparency, member ownership, community focus, innovation, and financial inclusion guide everything we do.

Our Story

25+ Years of Trust and Growth

Unity SACCO was founded in 1998 with a simple but powerful vision: to create a financial institution that truly serves its members' best interests. What started as a small cooperative with just 50 founding members has grown into one of Kenya's most trusted SACCOs.

Our journey has been marked by steady growth, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to our cooperative principles. Today, we proudly serve over 50,000 members across Kenya, providing them with comprehensive financial services and helping them build better futures for themselves and their families.
